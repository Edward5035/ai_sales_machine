5-Email Cold Outreach Sequence

Campaign Details

  • Industry: Professional Services (Customizable)
  • Sequence Length: 5 emails over 3 weeks
  • Open Rate: 25-35% expected
  • Response Rate: 8-12% expected
Email #1 - Initial Contact
Subject: Quick question about [COMPANY_NAME]'s growth plans
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I noticed [COMPANY_NAME] has been expanding in the [INDUSTRY] space, and I'm impressed by [SPECIFIC_COMPLIMENT].

I work with similar companies to help them [VALUE_PROPOSITION]. Just last month, I helped [SIMILAR_COMPANY] achieve [SPECIFIC_RESULT].

Would you be open to a brief 15-minute conversation about how we might be able to help [COMPANY_NAME] achieve similar results?

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
Email #2 - Value Add (3 days later)
Subject: [RESOURCE] for [COMPANY_NAME] - no strings attached
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I know you're busy, so I won't take much of your time.

I came across this [RESOURCE_TYPE] that specifically addresses [PAIN_POINT] for companies in the [INDUSTRY] space. Thought it might be valuable for [COMPANY_NAME].

[LINK_TO_RESOURCE]

No strings attached - just figured it could save you some time.

If you'd like to chat about how other companies are tackling this challenge, I'm happy to share what I've learned.

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
Email #3 - Social Proof (1 week later)
Subject: How [COMPETITOR/SIMILAR_COMPANY] solved this exact problem
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I was just working with [SIMILAR_COMPANY] (similar size to [COMPANY_NAME]), and they faced the exact same challenge with [SPECIFIC_PROBLEM].

Here's what we implemented:
• [SOLUTION_POINT_1]
• [SOLUTION_POINT_2]
• [SOLUTION_POINT_3]

Result: [SPECIFIC_MEASURABLE_RESULT] in just [TIME_FRAME].

I'd be happy to walk you through the exact process if you're interested. Would next Tuesday or Wednesday work for a quick call?

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
Email #4 - Urgency/Scarcity (1 week later)
Subject: Limited spots available - [COMPANY_NAME] interested?
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Quick heads up - I'm opening up [NUMBER] spots for new [SERVICE/PROGRAM] clients in [MONTH].

Given [COMPANY_NAME]'s focus on [RELEVANT_BUSINESS_GOAL], I think you'd be a perfect fit.

The program includes:
• [BENEFIT_1]
• [BENEFIT_2]
• [BENEFIT_3]

I've already got interest from [NUMBER] companies, so spots are filling up quickly.

Want to hop on a 15-minute call this week to see if it's a good fit?

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]
Email #5 - Final Follow-up (1 week later)
Subject: Last email about [MAIN_BENEFIT] for [COMPANY_NAME]
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

This will be my last email about [SERVICE/SOLUTION].

I understand you're busy and this might not be a priority right now - no worries at all.

But if [MAIN_PAIN_POINT] becomes a bigger issue for [COMPANY_NAME] down the road, feel free to reach out. I'll still be here to help.

In the meantime, I hope the [RESOURCE_FROM_EMAIL_2] I shared earlier was useful.

Wishing you and the team continued success!

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]

P.S. If you know anyone else in the [INDUSTRY] space who might benefit from what we do, I'd appreciate the connection. Happy to return the favor anytime.

Customization Variables

Replace these variables with specific information for each prospect:

  • [COMPANY_NAME]: Target company name
  • [FIRST_NAME]: Prospect's first name
  • [INDUSTRY]: Their industry/niche
  • [SPECIFIC_COMPLIMENT]: Recent achievement or growth
  • [VALUE_PROPOSITION]: Your main service offering
  • [YOUR_NAME]: Your full name and title
