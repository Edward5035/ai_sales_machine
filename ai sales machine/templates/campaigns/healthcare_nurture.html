Campaign Performance Tips

  • Personalization is key: Always use the patient's name and reference their specific health interests
  • Mobile-optimize: 70% of healthcare emails are opened on mobile devices
  • Trust signals: Include credentials, patient reviews, and professional certifications
  • Clear CTAs: Make booking appointments as simple as possible
  • Follow up: Continue nurturing non-responders with educational content

Healthcare-Specific Variables

  • [PRACTICE_NAME]: Name of the healthcare practice
  • [DOCTOR_NAME]: Primary healthcare provider name
  • [HEALTH_AREA]: Specialty (dental, cardiology, wellness, etc.)
  • [YEARS_EXPERIENCE]: Years in practice
  • [PHONE_NUMBER]: Direct appointment line
  • [BOOKING_LINK]: Online scheduling system
